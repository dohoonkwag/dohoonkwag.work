EARTI

Mobile Inspection Camera System

  • Collaborated with a team and developed a compact, convenient, and mobile inspection camera for Big S’s automated hydroponic farming system: EARTI
  • Utilized ESP32 microcontrollers to control stepper and servo motors, which allowed the camera to traverse a 70" height and pan 270+ degrees
  • The solution provided real-time and time-lapse visual data, integrated with Trolmaster software, and was waterproof, UV-tolerant, and non-corrosive for reliable surveillance inside of EARTI.

Eggcellent Imposter

Conservation Tech Egg

  • Worked with a team to develop a "tech egg" to help conservation efforts for the Attwater Prairie Chicken
  • The egg has the same dimensions and weight as a regular Attwater Prairie Chicken Egg
  • The "tech egg" utilizes an Arduino Nicla Sense ME and photoresistors to collect real-time data such as light exposure, temperature, and motion to better understand incubation patterns of this endangered species and improve egg viability.

Better Bottle

Feeding Solution for Babies with Cleft Lip/Palate

  • Collaborated with a team to develop a low-cost, food safe, easy-to-clean, and user-friendly feeding nipple for babies with cleft lip/palate that is affordable for affected families in San Cristóbal de las Casas.
  • Ensured the product was compatible with various baby bottle brands and meets food safety regulations.
  • Created an affordable baby bottle nipple with a simple design while also navigating existing patents on current solutions to ensure that the design does not infringe on intellectual property rights.
